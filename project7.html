"Oysters In My Pocket" Poster

Poster Design

2024

A poster design created for the Royel Otis song "Oysters In My Pocket." The poster's central image draws from the main visuals of the song itself, integrating an oyster with an image of the band to embrace a sense of surrealism that's part of the song and music video. The micro-typsetting within the poster is inspired by their existing branding which tends to use Helvetica and is often minimal in type sizes, choices and colors.
