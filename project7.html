"Tiny Moves" Poster

Poster Design

2024

Poster design inspired by the Bleachers song "Tiny Moves”. Drawing visual imagery from the name and lyrics of the song, the phrase "tiny moves" was reminiscent of a game of chess and a piece that stood out, replacing the white queen piece with a dancing figure from the song's music video. The typography is meant to be playful while still following a traditional path. The type is set into eight columns like the chess board and is justified to draw attention to the spaced out words that are emphasized in the song.
