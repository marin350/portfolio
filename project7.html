"Protect Print Media" Social Campaign

Poster Design

2024

A poster design created to advocate for print media and supporting local and independent newsstands. The visual language of the poster references the interactivity often found in newspapers through crosswords and word searches. Using the New York Times as a point of references, I designed the crossword to have strong contrast and integrate the message into the poster rather than have a block composition.
