NYC Historical Districts and Buildings Data Visualization

Web Design

2024

A data visualization using NYC Open Data’s API on the historical districts and individual landmarks of New York City. Due to the larger amount of data, the focus of the visualization was narrowed down to districts and buildings in Manhattan built between 1900-1999. The site represents two layers, the districts and the buildings within those districts. Each page contains the quantitative amount of buildings in each district and is further visually divided by the age of the building. Using opacity dynamically through JavaScript, the more faded or lighter the building the older it is. There is also an additional click even listener so when an individual building is clicked, more data is displayed about it.
