"All Of Us Strangers" Movie Poster

Poster Design

2023

Exploration of type as a primary visual, this poster uses type to express the interwoven nature of the film. The masked images within the type are intentionally placed in specific words that represent the relationship between the characters in the film as well as the way the film utilizes ideas of memory entwined in the past and present. The color palette draws reference from the film’s color scheme and lighting in key scenes.
