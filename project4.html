Dinner At Home

Print Design

2024

A saddle-stitch book design project that is a collection of both research and personal writing focused on the relationship between home, cooking and nostalgia on a personal level as well as within the larger context of the Indian diaspora through the lens of my immigrant parents. The book is divided into three parts: the research, an interview with my parents, and recipes for what dinner at my own home looks like. To keep visual connections throughout the book, a consistent typeface, image treatment and only 3 type sizes were used.
