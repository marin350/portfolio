In Loving Memory

Print Design

2024

A perfect bound book design project that is a collection of park bench plaques from Washington Square Park. The book is divided into 3 section: Love, Bear & Bird, and In Loving Memory, with each section containing found text relevant to the theme and images. The book uses a four column grid with quotes from the plaque overlapping the body text in the third column to the right. Another design system within the book is images are always on the left page and text always on the right.
