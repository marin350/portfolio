Quill Typeface

Type Design

2025

The typeface was inspired by woodcut typography paired with calligraphy as well as the intesection of typography and illustrations in playing cards. The design of Quill began with an ‘A’, found in the illustrated book ‘The Treatyse of Fysshynge wyth an Angle from the book of Saint Albans’ by Juliana Berners (1903). The left stroke of the ‘A’ became the backbone of desiging Quill—referencing calligraphy paired with woodcut type and a contemporary versatility to it.

The type specimen is a set of playing cards and a booklet based on the initial inspiration drawn from a deck of cards.
