Modular Typeface

Type Design

2023

The typeface was designed under a 5 x 5 grid only utilizing circles to create an upper and lowercase alphabet. The modular typeface followed the grid strictly, creating letterforms by removing circles from the grid. The x-height for the lowercase letterforms are at times inconsistent due to the odd number of circles in the grid seen in characters such as the “i” or “t” but it improves overall legibility by creating contrast between the characters.
