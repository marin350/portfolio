New York City Wiki-Book

Created as an assignment for Core 1 Typography at Parsons School of Design. The book is a documentation of New York City's Wikipedia page depicted through the use of a grid. The book embodies the sigifnicance of New York City's grid and is communicated through both the typography and the image treatments.

Typeface: Helvetica
