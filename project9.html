The Juggernaut

Visual Identity

2024

During my internship at The Juggernaut, a New York-based media company focused on telling powerful South Asian stories. I created the visual idenity for The Juggernaut Summit 2024, ranging from name tags, banners, digital signage to social media posts. Work outside of the summit consists of merch, promotional materials and digital design. The merch collection I designed titled "The Juggernaut Superstars" is focused on highligthing South Asian icons from the 1970's-90's.
