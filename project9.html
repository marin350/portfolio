The Juggernaut

Visual Identity

2024


The Juggernaut, a New York-based media company focused on telling powerful South Asian stories. During my internship, I created the visual identity for The Juggernaut Summit 2024, ranging from name tags, banners, digital signage to social media posts. Work outside of the summit consisted of merch, promotional materials and digital design. The merch collection I designed titled "The Juggernaut Superstars" was focused on highlighting South Asian icons from the 1970's-90's.
