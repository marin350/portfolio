Fluxus Manifesto Poster

Poster Design

2023

Using type as the primary visual, the poster aims to embody the key ideas of the Fluxus Manifesto as a movement, using phrases such as “non-art reality” to represent the movement’s emphasis on the making of art rather the the art itself. The poster uses overprinting as a method to challenge legibility and represent this idea of the “process.”
